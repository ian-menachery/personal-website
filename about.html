About My Journey

In my recent project, I applied ridge regression to predict IMDb ratings for movies based on various features, including box office earnings, Rotten Tomatoes scores, and movie genres. I addressed challenges like multicollinearity and overfitting through proper data transformation and model regularization.

The project involved several key steps:

  • Data Preprocessing
  • Modeling
  • Results
  • Assumptions Analysis

The results and insights gained from this project have deepened my understanding of regression analysis and its application in predicting real-world outcomes. I remain dedicated to furthering my expertise and welcome collaborations that may allow me to grow. Thank you for taking the time to explore my journey. I look forward to connecting with you.
