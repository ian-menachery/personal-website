About My Journey

I'm Ian Menachery, a dedicated professional specializing in data science and economics with a strong passion for leveraging data analytics to unravel economic insights and support strategic decision-making. My professional journey has equipped me with extensive experience in statistical modeling, econometrics, machine learning techniques, and data visualization. I pride myself on transforming intricate datasets into actionable strategies. In one recent project, I applied ridge regression to predict IMDb ratings for movies based on various features, including box office earnings, Rotten Tomatoes scores, and movie genres. I addressed challenges like multicollinearity and overfitting through proper data transformation and model regularization.

The project involved several key steps:

  • Data Preprocessing
  • Modeling
  • Results
  • Assumptions Analysis

The insights gained from this project have significantly deepened my understanding of regression analysis and its practical applications in predicting real-world outcomes. Beyond technical skills, I excel at clearly communicating complex analytical outcomes to diverse audiences. Whether teaching microeconomic theory, macroeconomics, or discrete mathematics, I prioritize clarity, collaboration, and real-world applicability. I'm continually driven by curiosity and a desire to tackle meaningful economic challenges. Explore my portfolio to view examples of my work, and please feel free to reach out for collaboration, networking opportunities, or simply to connect.
